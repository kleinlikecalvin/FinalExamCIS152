Tate's Brand Chocolate Chip Cookies Replica

Description

Yield: 6 dozen cookies. Tate's Bake Shop Chocolate Chip Cookies are thin and crispy, and absolutely delicious. Even if you prefer soft and chewy chocolate chip cookies, you'll find it hard to resist these buttery cookies filled with yummy chocolate chips and toasted pecans.

Ingredients

  • ½ cup salted butter (1 stick)
  • ⅜ cup (2.82 ounces) dark brown sugar
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ½ teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 cup (5 ounces) all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup semi-sweet chocolate chips
  • ½ cup toasted pecans

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350° F.
  2. Line cookie sheets with silicon or parchment paper; set aside.
  3. In a large bowl, cream the butter and sugars on medium speed until they come together, about 1 minute. Add the egg and vanilla.
  4. Beat on medium speed just until the mixture is combined, about 30 seconds. Add the salt and baking soda; beat on medium just until combined, about 30 seconds.
  5. Add the flour; beat on low just until combined, about 30 seconds. Do not over mix the dough.
  6. Fold in the chocolate chips and toasted pecans.
  7. Drop the cookies 2 inches apart onto the prepared cookie sheets using a small ice cream scoop.
  8. Flatten cookie dough to about ⅜-inch thick with a wet hand.
  9. Bake in preheated 350° F oven for 12 minutes or until the edges and centers are brown.
  10. Allow to cool for 5 minutes on cookie sheet.
  11. Remove the cookies to a wire rack to cool completely.
  12. Store in an air-tight container.
